Welcome to YoungMMA.inc!

YoungMMA.inc is a non-profit organization that helps kids in need by teaching them mixed martial arts. We teach kids respect and dicipline that will stay with them for the rest of their lives. This website is to help educate parents and anyone else who is unfamiliar with the sport of mixed martial arts to grasp how safe the sport really is.
